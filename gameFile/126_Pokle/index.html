Rules

  • 3 player Texas Holdem - Rules
  • Goal: Guess the 5 board cards - order within the flop does not matter!
  • Clues: You are shown all the players' hands and the Ranking Table - The Ranking Table shows the player ranks ( ) by hand value at the Flop, Turn and River
  • A green background means the guess is correct, a yellow background means the suit OR the rank is correct
  • There is only ONE correct solution - You need to guess a specific set of 5 cards even though many sets fit the ranking table.

Examples - Please follow carefully





IMPORTANT: Here, the King of Hearts is not yellow because the King of Spades has been guessed correctly

  • No kickers on the board - The rank of each board card will alway be a part of at least one player's hand in at least one phase.
    Note: "part of a hand" means the significant card(s) in: Pair, Two Pair, Three of a Kind or Four of a Kind (not kickers)

    TLDR: You may have to guess a card's suit with no clues, but not it's rank

  • Autocorrect - If you guess the correct solution except for the suit of the Turn and/or River, the suit(s) will be autocorrected and you will win. This mechanic exists to reduce the amount of blind guesswork in the game.
  • Tap guess cards to select them
  • Correct guesses (green) are autoguessed on the next row but can be changed
