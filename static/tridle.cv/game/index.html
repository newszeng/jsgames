TRIDLE - Triple Wordle
Try to guess all three words in 8 tries. Each guess is submitted by pressing the 'ENTER' key. Once the guess is submitted, each correct letter in the correct position is shown in green G, each correct letter in the incorrect position is shown in orange O and letters that are not in the word show up in black B.

The keys on the keyboard are split into thirds and colored indicate its status for each of the 3 puzzles
